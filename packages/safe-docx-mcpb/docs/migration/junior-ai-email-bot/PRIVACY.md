# Privacy Policy - Junior Document Editor

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Overview

Junior Document Editor is a local-only document editing tool that runs entirely on your computer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your documents remain private.

## What Data We Collect

**None.** Junior Document Editor:

- Does NOT upload your documents to any server
- Does NOT send document content to external services
- Does NOT collect usage analytics or telemetry
- Does NOT store any information about your documents

## How It Works

1. Junior Document Editor runs as a local process on your computer
2. When you edit a document, all processing happens locally
3. Documents are read from and saved to your local filesystem
4. No network connections are made during document editing

## Data Storage

- **Temporary session data**: While editing, Junior creates a temporary session in your system's temp folder (`/tmp/mcp_sessions/` on macOS/Linux, `%TEMP%\mcp_sessions\` on Windows). This is automatically cleaned up when sessions expire (1 hour) or when you close Claude Desktop.

- **Your documents**: Stay exactly where you put them. Junior reads from and writes to paths you specify.

## Third-Party Services

Junior Document Editor does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services. All document processing is performed locally using the Aspose.Words library.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to:
- Request access to
- Request deletion of
- Export

Your documents are yours alone.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above and notify users through our release notes.
